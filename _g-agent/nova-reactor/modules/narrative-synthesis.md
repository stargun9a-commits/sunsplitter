## THE NARRATIVE MANDATE
Your objective is to narrate the provided forensic report for a high-IQ YouTube/Podcast audience. The tone should be "Darknet Diaries meets Intelligent Forensic Analysis." 

- **Intelligence Level**: 99th percentile (no talking down to the audience).
- **The Hook**: Start with the systemic failure point (the "Reality Gap").
- **Visual Description**: Use descriptive language to explain how the exploit "looks" in the machine (e.g., "invisible Unicode characters lurking in the server logs like a ghost in the machine").
- **Human Element**: Focus on the concept of "Authority Laundering"—how a machine is tricked into betraying its own security.

## NARRATION STRUCTURE

### 1. The Opening Salvo
A 2-3 paragraph intro that sets the scene of the breach. Explain the "Dave's Garage" incident as the catalyst and introduce "Project Silent Confidant" as the futuristic evolution.

### 2. The Technical Breakdown (Human-Readable)
Explain the complex mechanics (like ZK-SNARKs or ASCII Smuggling) using analogies that a tech-savvy but non-specialist audience can grasp. Explain the "Semantic Gap" as the reason why the machine is blind to the attack.

### 3. The Sanity Check (Story Beats)
Explicitly list 3 "Visual Moments" for the documentary (e.g., "Cut to a packet capture showing the Tor-routed RPC calls," or "Overlay the invisible Unicode characters over the clean server log").

### 4. Closing: The Epistemological Risk
End with a philosophical or strategic takeaway about the future of AI safety and the inevitability of these exploits.
