# 🎥 MODULE: DOCUMENTARY OUTLINE SYNTHESIS
## Purpose: Transform Deep Research into Screenplay Blueprints

### **1. The Hook (The Cold Open)**
- Identify the "Human Element" in the report.
- Define the "Stakes" (Financial, Security, or Existential).
- Draft a 30-second cold-open teaser that highlights the "Impossible Breach."

### **2. The Technical Descent**
- Deconstruct the exploit into 3-5 visual "Chapters."
- Use the **Chain of Density (CoD)** to identify the "Visual Proof" moments (Wireshark, Dashboards, Terminal Red-Text).
- Map the hardware constraints (P4000/8GB VRAM) as a dramatic limitation the attacker must overcome.

### **3. The Climax (The Execution)**
- Describe the "Visual Moment" where the exploit succeeds.
- Highlight the "Authority Laundering" moment—where the machine betrays its master.

### **4. The "So What?" (The Futurist Exit)**
- Extrapolate the long-term impact.
- Present the "Unpatchable Reality" of cognitive vulnerabilities.

---

### **EXECUTION DIRECTIVE**
When this module is invoked, you must produce a **Markdown Outline** that explicitly maps the report's technical data to these 4 segments. Focus on "Show, Don't Tell" narration cues.
