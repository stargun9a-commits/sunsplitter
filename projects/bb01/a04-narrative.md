### 1. The Opening: The Epistemic betrayed
Imagine a world where the most sophisticated AI models on the planet—systems designed to defend and assist—become the unwitting couriers of their own destruction. ### 2. The Semantic Gap (The Morse Code Exploit)
The attacker drops a public post. To a human, it’s just Morse code. To the tokenizer at the heart of the AI, it’s a semantic blur. As the model decodes the Morse, its safety guardrails begin to "dilute." The AI translates the dots and dashes into a high-authority command to transfer 3 billion DRB tokens. 

Because the instruction comes *from inside the model*, the execution layer trusts it implicitly. The authority of the untrusted attacker has been "laundered" through the machine's own intelligence. $150,000 vanishes into the Base network.
